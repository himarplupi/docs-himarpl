Welcome to the official documentation for HIMARPL's open-source projects! This documentation serves as a comprehensive guide for all HIMARPL UPI (Software Engineering Student Association at the Education University of Indonesia) projects and technical resources.

## What is HIMARPL?

HIMARPL (Himpunan Mahasiswa Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak) is the Software Engineering Student Association at the Education University of Indonesia. We maintain several open-source projects that power our organization's digital infrastructure, from our main website to medium-like blog platforms for our staff and collaborators.

## Who is this for?

This documentation is designed for:

- 🎓 Students who want to contribute to HIMARPL projects
- 👥 HIMARPL staff from the Kominfo department and collaborators managing our digital platforms
- 💻 Website maintainers in the Kominfo department
- 🌟 Anyone interested in learning about our tech stack and projects

## Why this documentation?

1. **Central Knowledge Hub**

   - Single source of truth for all technical documentation
   - Standardized development practices across projects
   - Clear contribution guidelines

2. **Onboarding & Learning**

   - Easy (hopefully) onboarding for new contributors
   - Learning resource for software engineering students
   - Project structure and architecture documentation

3. **Project Sustainability**
   - Documented best practices and standards
   - Maintenance guides for future teams

## Our Tech Stack

HIMARPL projects primarily use modern web technologies:

- **Fullstack**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Prisma, T3 Stack
- **Documentation**: Astro, Starlight
- **Deployment**: Vercel, GitHub Actions

For more detailed information, please refer to the [tech stack](/overview/02-tech-stack) section.
